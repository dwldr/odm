Note

DMV will charge a test fee each time you take a test. You must pay test fees in advance. Please bring a separate payment for your issuance fee. For example, bring two checks: one for your test(s) and one for your license or permit.

Payment must be cash or check only.

DMV does not accept debit or credit cards.

Fees are subject to change. Visit DMV's website at www.OregonDMV.com for current information.
